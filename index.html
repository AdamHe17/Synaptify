How does it work?

The tempo, key, tone, and notes of your musical fingerprint are generated based on the unique characteristics of the ridges (the curved lines that show up in a fingerprint image) in your finger:

  • "spread": the average distance between ridges
  • "splits": the event that a ridge splits into two
  • "ends": the even that a ridge discontinues
